Recipin – Save Recipes in One Click

Recipin is a modern, minimal Chrome extension designed to make saving recipes effortless. Just type a name and hit save — the extension automatically stores the page URL using the Chrome API. With its clean and intuitive interface, Recipin keeps your favorite cooking ideas organized and just a click away. Simple, sleek, and built for everyday use.
